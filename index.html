"Tears of Blue and Gold"

A Poem by Lucas Alven

As rain falls down from the sky
I remember my past with Blue tears in my eye

A past where I never felt like enough
Looking back those times were tough

Feelings of numbness were crappy
As I navigated to find what was happy

I suffered through that pain
But I must not take that time in vain

Because past feelings can make you want to run away
But they are actually what make you stronger today

As the sun always clears the rain
The same can be said of pain

Those feelings begin to seem old
Now I have tears of joy that are Gold

I have finally found my place
Crossed the line of this endless race

No longer do I feel alone
Anxiety fades from within my bones

So now all here have been told
This is the story of my tears that are Blue and Gold
